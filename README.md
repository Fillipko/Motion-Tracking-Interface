Instructions:
-------------
Place your hand in front of the camera on your computer making one of the gestures to preform the following commands-  
- Open Hand --> Zoom In  
- Closed Hand --> Zoom Out  
- Thumb Up --> Scroll Up  
- Thumb Down --> Scroll Down
